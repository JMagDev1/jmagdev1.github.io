HyperDrive Havoc

Once the user opens the game this is the first screen they are presented with, once they press play they can then progress onto the main menu (Shown below).

The car selection menu is shown on here, the user can cycle through the 4 available cars by using the left and right arrows.

This is the main menu, it contains all of the maps, all the user has to do is click on it and then select a car from the car selection screen shown below.

These are the 4 available cars that are in the game, they are able to be selected by pressing the play button at the top of the screen.

Once you load into the game you will see this screen, depending on what map was selected you will see different AI cars each level and also each car will go at different speeds so that it will vary the difficulty of the levels.
